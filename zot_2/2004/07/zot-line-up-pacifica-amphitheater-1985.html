Zot 2
Back in the good old days you could drop into the local record shop and pick up an album from a band with a weird name...and it would be great. The self entitled Zot album by the band named Zot was one of those great finds of the 80s. (top banner photo features [from left to right] Chris Stewart, Tom Calderaro, Bryant Simpson, Randy Wayne, Andy Thomas, Pat Knowles. Circa 1985.)

I remember meeting Pat when I was twelve years old. He blistered out a rendition of " Cat scratch fever" complete with lead. We went to eighth grade together at Circle View. I was jealous of his constant positivity and optimistic out look on life. Though I haven't seen Pat in a few years I would find myself wondering where life's path has taken him. I give him credit for my never playing guitar, for after seeing him play at such a young age I knew that I could never be anywhere as good. I stuck to sports instead. Pats probably exchanging licks with Hendrix, Vaughn, Allmen and crew. God never seams to have enough great guitar players. Thanks Pat, you'll be dearly missed. When we met up again can I be greeted at " The Gates " with you playing "Cat's Scratch Fever"?
