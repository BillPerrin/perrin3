Zot 2
Back in the good old days you could drop into the local record shop and pick up an album from a band with a weird name...and it would be great. The self entitled Zot album by the band named Zot was one of those great finds of the 80s. (top banner photo features [from left to right] Chris Stewart, Tom Calderaro, Bryant Simpson, Randy Wayne, Andy Thomas, Pat Knowles. Circa 1985.)

MTV interview - premiere of "Uranium" video

By Bill on June 12, 2005 2:47 PM | 4 Comments

MTV's Mark Goodman interviewed the band for the first ever showing of their Uranium video. This is a dated video taken from an old VHS tape and the quality is not the best. I'd like to thank Zot fan Jim Hewitt for passing this along.

There are a few spots of the interview missing, but the majority of it is intact. Randy Wayne handles most of the questions, but Bryant Simpson and Patrick Knowles get to jump in as well (Chris Stewart doesn't speak).

The entire interview runs three and a half minutes (there appears to be a bit more that was lost). The Uranium video is not included in this clip. I have a much better copy of the video that I'm in the process of digitizing.
