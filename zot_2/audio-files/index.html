Zot 2
Back in the good old days you could drop into the local record shop and pick up an album from a band with a weird name...and it would be great. The self entitled Zot album by the band named Zot was one of those great finds of the 80s. (top banner photo features [from left to right] Chris Stewart, Tom Calderaro, Bryant Simpson, Randy Wayne, Andy Thomas, Pat Knowles. Circa 1985.)
